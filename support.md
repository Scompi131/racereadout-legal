# Support — Race Readout

**Race Readout** is a motorsport timing and telemetry viewer built for fans who want to understand a race, not just watch it.

If you have a question or need help, email us at **alberto.scomparin@live.com** and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### The app shows no data — what do I do?

Race Readout fetches live data from the public [OpenF1 API](https://openf1.org). Make sure you have an active internet connection when using the app. If the API is temporarily unavailable, try again in a few minutes.

### How do I view timing or telemetry?

1. From the home screen, tap **Timing** or **Telemetry**.
2. Use the picker menus to cascade through: **Season → Event → Session**.
3. For Telemetry, continue selecting **Driver → Run → Lap**.
4. Charts and analysis will load automatically once your selections are complete.

### Which sessions are available?

Any session recorded in the OpenF1 database is available — this includes practice, qualifying, sprint, and race sessions from 2023 onwards.

### Does the app require an account or login?

No. Race Readout has no accounts, no sign-in, and no registration. Just open the app and go.

### Is the app free?

Yes, every feature is free. There is an optional in-app tip if you'd like to support development — it unlocks nothing, it's just a thank-you.

### What data does the app show in Timing?

The Timing section loads a complete session and shows:
- Full session results with lap times
- Lap-time chart across the entire session
- Sector time breakdown per driver
- Pace comparison between drivers
- Pit stop timing and strategy
- Race progression (position over laps)
- Stint analysis by tyre compound

### What data does the app show in Telemetry?

The Telemetry section lets you pick a specific lap and shows:
- Speed, throttle, brake, gear, RPM, and DRS traces
- Derived channels: longitudinal g-force, lateral g-force, and corner curvature
- Track path reconstructed in an inertial frame from positional data
- Side-by-side lap or run comparisons

### I found a bug or want to suggest a feature

Please email **alberto.scomparin@live.com** with a description of the issue or suggestion. Include the session you were viewing (season, event, session type) if the issue is data-related.
