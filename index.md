# Privacy Policy — Race Readout

**Effective date:** 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Race Readout** mobile application ("the App") handles
information. The App is developed and published by **Alberto Scomparin** ("we", "us"), an
individual developer.

In short: **Race Readout does not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

---

## 1. Information we collect

**None.** The App has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no registration. We do not collect,
store, or transmit any personally identifiable information about you. We do not use analytics,
advertising, or tracking technologies of any kind.

## 2. Data stored on your device

The App saves a small number of **preferences locally on your device only** — for example your
chosen lap-time format, your favourite driver, and the season you last viewed. This information
never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or anyone else, and is removed if you delete
the App.

## 3. Network requests

To show timing and telemetry information, the App retrieves **publicly available motorsport
data** from the third-party **OpenF1 API** (<https://openf1.org>) over a secure (HTTPS)
connection. These requests are necessary for the App to function.

As with any internet request, the receiving service may observe standard technical information
such as your device's IP address. This is handled by OpenF1 under its own terms and privacy
practices; **we do not receive, log, or store any of this information.** We have no control over
OpenF1's data practices and encourage you to review their terms.

## 4. Advertising and tracking

The App contains **no advertising** and performs **no tracking**. We do not use any advertising
identifier (such as Apple's IDFA or the Android Advertising ID) and do not track you across apps
or websites.

## 5. In-app purchases

The App is free, and every feature is available at no cost. The App offers an **optional tip**
to support development. Tips are **entirely voluntary and unlock no features or content.**

Any tip you choose to make is **processed by the app store you downloaded the App from** (the
Apple App Store or Google Play) through its standard in-app purchase system. **We never see or
store your payment details.** The store provides us only with anonymized, aggregated sales
information (such as totals), which does not identify you. Your purchase is governed by that
store's terms and privacy policy.

## 6. Children's privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It is a general-audience
application and is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal
information from children.

## 7. Third-party services

- **OpenF1 API** — public source of the motorsport data displayed in the App
  (<https://openf1.org>).
- **Apple / Google** — the app store that distributes the App (the Apple App Store or Google
  Play) and processes any optional tip may collect information in accordance with its own privacy
  policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## 8. Data security

Because the App does not collect or store personal data on our servers (we operate none), there
is no personal data for us to secure. All network communication with the OpenF1 API uses
encrypted HTTPS connections.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with
an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes
acceptance of the revised policy.
